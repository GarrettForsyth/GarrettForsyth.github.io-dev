About Me

I am an aspiring Android developer located in the Waterloo region, ON. I graduated from the University of Waterloo with a degree in honours physics, specializing in astrophysics.

I taught myself the fundamentals of software development through resources such as Coursera and Saylor Academy. I then worked through a course on full stack web development using Ruby on Rails offered by The Odin Project. Finally I taught myself Android through Google's Developer Training and courses offered through Udacity, eventually earning the Associate Android Developer Certification! Along the way, I reinforced the concepts by creating toy projects, some listed below and many more which can be found at my github.

One of the most attractive things about software development, is the power to make your creations easily accessible to a large amount of people. With the right idea and execution, you can really make an impact in people's lives. I find great meaning in that, and it's my goal to put myself in a position where my work and ideas can help people.
